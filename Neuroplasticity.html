Neuroplasticity and the Growth Mindset.

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.

When we learn, we create new pathways between neurons in our brains. We often get caught in a trap of saying "I'm good at X but I'm terrible at Y" and that becomes a fixed concept to us. Neuroplasticity means that "even though I'm currently terrible at Y, I can learn about it and get better at it, it isn't something that I have to live with forever. I am not the issue, I just haven't been exposed to it enough". The value of understanding Neuroplasticity is that anyone can learn anything.

Discuss how you might engage with the principals of neuroplasticity for your own benefit.

As I'm currently learning to code as a part of the Enspiral Dev Academy, understanding neuroplasticity allows me to get past self doubts and negativity in my learning process. Proficiency with a skill doesnt happen in 2 weeks.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

  • Get enough quality sleep.
  • Keep active and excercise.
  • Find purpose in what you're doing.
  • Read books.

How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

  • Celebrate growth with others. This will mean giving honest and true praise to others in my cohort and being able to accept it when it's given to me.
  • Emphasise growth over speed. recognise that I won't be the fastest to learn if I want to learn it well. I will and should make mistakes to grow from them.
  • Be realistic in my expectations of myself. Its going to take a lot longer than this bootcamp for me to be a brilliant developer but it will give me a strong start and help me find a growth path.
